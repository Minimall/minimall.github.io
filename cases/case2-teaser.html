Cinderblock is a job management platform helping contractors run their businesses efficiently. I joined their team as Lead Product Designer to guide a complete platform redesign ahead of a major marketing push.

  • Startups
  • Product Design

In early 2023, I partnered with the founding team to modernize their Ember.js-based product, focusing on the complex Estimates & Invoices section. Within three months, I built a comprehensive design system with documented components and interactive prototypes that respected technical constraints.

The project resulted in a scalable design foundation that supported Cinderblock's growth plans while maintaining their core value of practical usability. My work established patterns for future product development in the contractor-focused software space.

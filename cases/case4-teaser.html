WOMBI is a Web3 marketing analytics platform helping crypto projects understand their audience.

I've joined their founding team as the first Product Designer to help launch the product from 0 to 1.

  • Scale-ups
  • Mature organisations

In early 2022, when Web3 was at its peak, my founder friend invited me to help shape their vision for a better analytics platform. Working with a small team of engineers, I took ownership of the entire product experience: from crafting the strategy and core UX, to building a practical design system with TailwindUI, to creating a unique brand identity featuring AI-generated wombat mascots.

We launched the MVP in just 6 months, gaining our first paying customers and growing community engagement. While the crypto winter affected the platform's growth trajectory, we proved that a small team could ship a complex analytics product efficiently.

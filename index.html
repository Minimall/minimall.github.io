About me

Background

I got into design early — back in school in Kharkiv, where I was making websites from HTML tutorials and spending hours trying to understand how software works. Later, I helped build first digital e-commerce platforms for retail leaders in Ukraine, redesigned airline check-ins in Dublin, and worked on games played by millions of people.

My background lets me design with feasibility in mind, speak devs' language, and help teams avoid dead-end decisions early.

I don't just make things look good — I make them buildable. I thrive at the intersection of design, product, and engineering. Especially when designing:

  • Early-stage MVPs and startup concepts (0→1 product design)
  • AI agents and tools
  • Tools for technical users (analysts, clinicians, developers, product teams)

I've had the pleasure of working with companies like WCG, HP Enterprise, and Ryanair.

I helped them build their products from scratch and improve existing ones. I worked on prototypes, design systems, high-fidelity UI, interaction design, and much more.

How I work

I combine hands-on execution with design leadership, working flexibly across roles depending on the stage of the team.

I often embed deeply — helping define strategy, prototype early concepts, scale design systems, and guide hiring or team development.

I move fast with prototypes, prefer feedback over perfection, and believe the best outcomes come from shared ownership and open iteration.

My current interests include designing for technical audiences, experimenting with generative tools, and exploring the intersection of design and code.

I'm always looking for projects where clarity, impact, and creativity can coexist.

I work best with:

  • Founders with raw ideas who need a fast, functional design to validate or pitch
  • Product teams looking to level up usability and polish
  • Engineering-led companies that want a designer who gets code
  • Teams building tools for other builders
